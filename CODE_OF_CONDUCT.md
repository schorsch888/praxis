# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Please read the full text at the link above. By participating in this project, you agree to abide by its terms.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers by [opening a private issue](https://github.com/praxis-skills/praxis/issues/new?labels=conduct&title=Code+of+Conduct+Report) or contacting a maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
